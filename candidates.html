Every submission through the issue form lands here before it's part of the public catalog. "In review" means it passed automatic validation and has an open draft pull request. "Needs fixing" means validation caught a problem and no pull request exists yet — usually a blank required field or a dropdown answer that didn't match exactly.

AmazoniaDB is an independent index, not a data host. Source names and materials remain with their respective providers.
