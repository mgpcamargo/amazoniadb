How AmazoniaDB works

We index. Providers publish.

Each card points to the original publisher’s page or repository. AmazoniaDB stores descriptive catalog metadata, not the underlying files.

  1. Source first.Every entry names and links to the institution that publishes it.
  2. Terms stay visible.Use conditions belong to each provider and must be checked before reuse.
  3. Care around sensitive data.We do not list precise sensitive locations or information that could put people, territories, or species at risk.

Growing responsibly

A directory designed to grow to thousands of sources.

New entries should include a direct publisher link, geographic coverage, a short plain-language description, access conditions, and a review date. This keeps the catalog useful without copying data that does not belong here.
